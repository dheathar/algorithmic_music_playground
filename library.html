Sounds from space

Real plasma- and radio-wave recordings made by University of Iowa instruments aboard spacecraft (Voyager, Cluster, Van Allen Probes and more), curated by Prof. Don Gurnett. The "sounds" are electromagnetic waves shifted into our hearing range — the actual voices of the planets' magnetospheres. Free to use under CC BY 4.0; press play, and see how each might enhance an organ.

Source: space-audio.org — University of Iowa plasma wave group (D. A. Gurnett). Licensed CC BY 4.0. Files are stored locally in space_library/. Attribute the source if you publish a track that uses them.
