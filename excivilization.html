Excivilization

A civilization as a piece of music — Greece across 2,800 years. The thesis: the civilization is a melody, and history is everything that tries (and fails) to erase it. One theme is born, and every empire that conquers Greece ends up speaking Greek.

▶ Watch the visualization →

The glue — two threads across the ages

the eternal drone (one D, unbroken) fear (the unknown) wisdom (experience) the theme recurs

The spine: the newborn's purity turns to fear, unaware of what lies beyond; across the ages the fear grows — but it is outweighed by the experience and wisdom the civilization accumulates. Fear is never erased; wisdom holds it in balance.
