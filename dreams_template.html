Dream seeds

Real, anonymized dreams from DreamBank, gathered by theme. These are inspiration only — read one, feel it, then tell Jarre seed a creation from <id> and we synthesize something wholly our own. The text stays local and is never used in a master. To add a theme: dreams_fetch.py --theme "Name" "query".
