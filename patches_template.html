Patches & harmony

A bank of ready voices in our own engine format — organ, Jarre leads, Vangelis brass, bass, bells — each with an audio preview and the recipe. Synthesized from scratch (no presets/samples); the usable JSON lives in patches/bank/. Below the bank: the rules of harmony & combination for building with them. Render any patch live with the server: POST /render, or render_patch(load_patch('patches/bank/<id>.json')).
